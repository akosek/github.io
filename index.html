About

We are 3 students from DTU and we would like to share with you our work, which was made for the purposes of
the Social Graphs And Interactions course in fall 2017.

You will find here different information about the process of exploring data of music artists and their songs, which we used to built networks of similar artists and analyzed emotional attachment to their songs.

For the beggingin play around and type the name of the artist or band of your choice. Find out where are they the most popular and what are the similar artisits to them.
