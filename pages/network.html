Network

We explored music in 14 countries, including our home-countries and other geographical locations that we were intrested for analysis. By that extend we managed to structure a one global network, so we could have a better and more clear perception of conections between artists and their different type of music. Artists plays the role of nodes (up to 150) and each node is connected to another one, determined by their similarity (Edges) to other artist, that we got from LastFm API.

For example by those results we could discover that in all of our 14 countries the band Radiohead is one of the most popular between others. Find out more by yourself!
