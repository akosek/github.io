Network

We explored music in 14 countries, including our home-countries and other geographical locations that we believe our data analysis would be better and more efficient. By that extend we managed to structure a one global network, so we could have a better and more clear perception of conections between artists and their different type of music. As far as our network is concerned, artists plays the role of nodes (150) and each node is connected to another one, determined by their similarity (Edges) to other artist.

Music Networks for 14 countries

We built seperated networks for 14 picked countries in order to see which are the most popular there and what are the communities of different type of music we can find inside. Play around!
