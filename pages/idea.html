Meet Our Data

The main idea of our project is to perform sentiment analysis for countires and artists based on their music. We are looking for a smilraity connections beetwen them based on the type of their music and emmotions they bring with their songs. First, we started from analysis of the API data file from LastFm API,
which includes information for each music artist about:

  • Tags describing style of his music
  • Locations where is he mostly listened
  • Up to 50 top music tracks he has
  • List of artists similar to him

This made possible for us to create 14 networks for picked countries and finally a main global network which is built from all the artists (450) in our database and represents a worldwide music map. More detailed information about our network, you can find in the Network section.

Understand our dataset

Lyrics data

Our second data source comes from Heroku API at Github, that returns lyrics after searching on lyrics.wikia.com based on Artist and Song. We collected data including up to 50 songs lyrics of each artist in dataset from LastFM to create his vocabulary and determine the sentiment level of each country by analyzing the most listened songs there.
